We offer instruction for golfers of all skill levels, specializing in introducing youngsters to the game.

SNAG

Twin Oaks is a certified teaching facility for the SNAG (Starting New At Golf) program, making it fun and rewarding for kids and grown-ups to play the game.

Unique clubs, balls, tees are used — and targets are set up on the course, which may simultaneously host a full game of pitch and putt.

For more information on SNAG, visit the official website at www.snaggolf.com.
