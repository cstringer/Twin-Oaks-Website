Welcome!

Twin Oaks Golf Complex is a unique golf facility serving Lawrence and Eudora, Kansas and the surrounding area.

We invite golfers of all skill levels to practice and play at our friendly facility — featuring a full driving range, the 9-hole Wakarusa Pitch & Putt course, professional instruction, new and used equipment sales, and a special focus on junior golf.

Twin Oaks is a certified SNAG (Starting New At Golf) instruction facility, providing a fun, accessible way to learn and play for youngsters and grown-ups alike.
